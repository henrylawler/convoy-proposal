Core Skills
01

Property Scanning

Automated monitoring of CoStar, county assessor records, LoopNet, Crexi, and public filings. Filtered to target criteria. New matches surfaced daily via automated alerts.

02

Deal Pipeline

Every match enters a structured pipeline - status tracking, priority scoring, notes, activity history. Integrates with existing CRM or runs standalone. Replaces spreadsheet workflows.

03

Owner Intelligence

Entity lookups via county records, Secretary of State filings, and contact enrichment APIs (RocketReach, Apollo, public registrar data). Owner names, mailing addresses, phone, email - cross-referenced for accuracy.

04

Outreach Generation

AI-drafted outreach referencing the specific property, recent comparable sales, and local market context. Generates email drafts, direct mail templates, and call prep sheets.

Additional AI Capability Options

Owner Intent Signals

AI monitors 20+ public data signals that predict seller motivation before a property hits the market:

  • Properties held 10+ years with no recent refinance
  • Out-of-state owners (management burden)
  • Expiring commercial leases
  • Recent ownership entity changes, probate filings
  • Code violations, deferred maintenance
  • Tax delinquencies, pre-foreclosure filings

Sources: county tax assessor records, Secretary of State filings, court records, building permit databases, USPS change-of-address data.

Comparable Sales Analysis

Automated comps pulled from county records and listing platforms - recent sales within target radius, price-per-SF trends, cap rate movements. Included in outreach and pipeline scoring.

CRM Integration

Multi-touch outreach sequencing - email, direct mail triggers, call prep sheets with talking points per property. Tracks response status, follow-up timing, and adapts cadence based on engagement. Integrates with existing CRM or built standalone.

Market Reports

Automated briefings on target markets - new listings, recent trades, rent trends, vacancy shifts, zoning changes. Data sourced from CoStar API, county records, Crexi, and municipal planning databases.
